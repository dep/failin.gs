failin.gs: don't worry. nobody's perfect

failin.gs seeks to answer that age old question that perhaps all of us have asked ourselves at some point in our lives.

"What is wrong with me?"

Do you talk too much? Are you selfish? Do you come off as arrogant? Maybe you have some sort of weird mannerism that you don't even know about! Nobody is perfect. We all have our own idiosyncrasies. Well now maybe you can learn about them from your friends -- all anonymously!

Create a profile and invite people to leave anonymous constructive criticism about your character. We all want to improve, don't we?

Can you handle the truth?
